Leslie Knope

The Pride of Pawnee

Photo of Leslie Knope. Library of Congress.
  • 1975: Leslie Knope is born on January 18th in Eagleton, Indiana. (Ms. Knope would like to highlight that this was out of her control and she was quickly transported to and raised in the greatest city in the world, Pawnee.)
  • 1985: Robert Knope, Leslie's father, dies.
  • 1989: Knope enrolls at Pawnee North High School where she becomes a member of student organizations such as The Debate Club and Model U.N.
  • 1993: Leslie graduates in the top 5% of her class. Enters Indiana University in the fall.
  • 1997: Knope graduates summa cum laude from Indiana University's School of Public and Environmental Affairs.
  • 2005: Leslie begins working as the Deputy Director of the Pawnee Parks Department.
